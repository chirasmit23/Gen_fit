Glute Kickback

Description

The Glute Kickback is an isolation exercise that targets the gluteus maximus. It's often performed on a cable machine or using bodyweight and is great for strengthening and toning the glutes while improving hip stability.

Posture Guide

- Attach an ankle strap to a low pulley cable and fasten it around one ankle
- Support yourself by holding onto the machine and keep your torso stable
- Slowly kick the strapped leg backward and upward in an arc
- Squeeze your glute at the top of the movement
- Lower your leg with control and repeat before switching sides
