Barbell Back Squat

Description

The barbell back squat is a foundational compound movement that targets the quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, and lower back. It is essential for developing lower body strength, stability, and muscle mass.

Posture Guide

- Place the barbell across your upper traps, not your neck
- Keep feet shoulder-width apart with toes slightly outward
- Brace your core and maintain a neutral spine
- Lower down by bending at the hips and knees until thighs are parallel
- Drive through your heels to return to the standing position
