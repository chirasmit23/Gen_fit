Leg Extension

Description

The Leg Extension is an isolation exercise that targets the quadriceps. It is typically performed using a leg extension machine and is ideal for building quad size, improving definition, and strengthening the knee joint.

Posture Guide

- Sit on the leg extension machine with your back flat and knees aligned with the pivot point
- Hook your feet under the padded bar
- Extend your legs fully while squeezing your quads at the top
- Lower the weight in a controlled motion back to the starting position
- Avoid swinging or using momentum to lift the weight
