Hip Thrust

Description

The Hip Thrust is a powerful compound exercise that targets the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back. It is excellent for improving hip extension strength and developing glute size and strength.

Posture Guide

- Sit on the ground with your upper back against a bench and a barbell positioned over your hips
- Bend your knees and place your feet flat on the ground, shoulder-width apart
- Drive through your heels to lift your hips until your thighs are parallel to the ground
- Squeeze your glutes at the top, keeping your core engaged
- Lower your hips back down with control and repeat
