Heel-Elevated Goblet Squat

Description

The Heel-Elevated Goblet Squat is a quad-dominant lower body exercise that increases range of motion and improves knee tracking by elevating the heels. Holding a dumbbell or kettlebell in front (goblet position) adds core activation and posture reinforcement.

Posture Guide

- Stand with your heels elevated on weight plates or a wedge, feet shoulder-width apart
- Hold a dumbbell or kettlebell at chest height in the goblet position
- Keep your chest up and core tight
- Squat down, allowing your knees to travel forward while keeping heels elevated
- Go as deep as possible with a straight spine
- Drive through your midfoot to return to standing
- Avoid letting your elbows drop below the knees during the squat
