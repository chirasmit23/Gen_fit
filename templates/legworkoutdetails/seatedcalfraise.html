Seated Calf Raise

Description

The Seated Calf Raise targets the soleus muscle of the calf and is a key exercise for lower leg development.

Posture Guide

- Sit on the calf raise machine with your feet on the foot platform
- Place your thighs under the pads
- Push through the balls of your feet to raise your heels
- Pause at the top and squeeze your calves
- Lower your heels slowly to stretch the calves
