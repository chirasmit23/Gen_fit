Cable Lateral Raise

Description

The Cable Lateral Raise is a resistance-based shoulder exercise that targets the lateral deltoid muscles. It offers constant tension throughout the movement, helping to develop shoulder width and symmetry.

Posture Guide

- Stand next to a low cable pulley with a handle attached
- Grasp the handle with the hand farthest from the pulley
- Keep your back straight and core tight
- Raise your arm laterally to shoulder height while keeping a slight bend in your elbow
- Slowly lower the handle back to the starting position
- Perform equal reps on both sides
