Half-Reps Lateral Raise

Description

The Half-Reps Lateral Raise is a shoulder burnout technique focusing on the medial deltoid. It involves performing lateral raises with short range of motion to maintain tension on the muscles, often done after full reps to push the muscle beyond fatigue.

Posture Guide

- Stand upright holding dumbbells at your sides
- Raise the dumbbells out to the sides only halfway up (about 45 degrees)
- Lower back to starting position without relaxing the tension
- Maintain slight bend in elbows and avoid using momentum
- Perform high reps with continuous partial range of motion for a deep burn
