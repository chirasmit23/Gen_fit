Upright Row Burnout

Description

The Upright Row Burnout is an intense high-repetition finisher that targets the deltoids and traps. This burnout variation is performed at the end of a shoulder or upper-body workout to fully fatigue the muscles using a lighter weight and minimal rest.

Posture Guide

- Stand tall with feet shoulder-width apart
- Hold a barbell or dumbbells with an overhand grip in front of your thighs
- Pull the weight straight up towards your chin, keeping elbows higher than wrists
- Pause briefly at the top, then slowly lower back down
- Perform high reps with strict form and controlled motion until failure
