Machine Lateral Raise

Description

The Machine Lateral Raise targets the lateral deltoid muscles using a fixed motion path to reduce form breakdown and allow for safer isolation of the shoulders. It is great for beginners and effective for building shoulder width.

Posture Guide

- Sit upright in the machine with your back against the pad
- Adjust the seat so your arms align with the machineâ€™s arms
- Grip the handles or place elbows against the pads (based on design)
- Raise your arms outward to shoulder height, squeezing the delts
- Lower slowly and repeat for the desired reps
