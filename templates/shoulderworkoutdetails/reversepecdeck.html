Reverse Pec Deck

Description

The Reverse Pec Deck targets the rear deltoids and upper back muscles. This machine-based movement is excellent for isolating the posterior delts, promoting better posture and balanced shoulder development.

Posture Guide

- Sit facing the machine with your chest against the pad
- Adjust the seat height so the handles are at shoulder level
- Grab the handles with a neutral or overhand grip
- Pull the handles outward and back in a reverse fly motion
- Squeeze your shoulder blades together at peak contraction
- Return with control and repeat
