Incline Reverse Fly

Description

The Incline Reverse Fly is an isolation exercise targeting the rear deltoids and upper back. It's performed on an incline bench, allowing a greater range of motion and focus on shoulder stability and posture.

Posture Guide

- Set an incline bench at about 30â€“45 degrees
- Lie chest-down on the bench with dumbbells in both hands
- Let your arms hang down naturally
- With a slight bend in the elbows, raise the dumbbells out to the sides
- Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top
- Lower the weights slowly back to the start position
