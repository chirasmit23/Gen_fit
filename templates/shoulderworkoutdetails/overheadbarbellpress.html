Overhead Barbell Press

Description

The Overhead Barbell Press, also known as the Military Press, is a fundamental shoulder exercise that targets the deltoids, triceps, and upper chest. It's excellent for building upper body strength and enhancing shoulder stability.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and grip the barbell just outside shoulder width
- Start with the barbell at collarbone level with elbows slightly in front of the bar
- Press the barbell overhead in a straight line while keeping your core tight
- Avoid arching your lower back and lock out your elbows at the top
- Lower the bar slowly back to the starting position
