Push Press

Description

The Push Press is a compound movement that primarily targets the shoulders, triceps, and upper chest, while also engaging the lower body to drive the weights overhead. It allows you to press more weight than a strict shoulder press by utilizing leg drive.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell at shoulder height with a firm grip
- Keep your chest up, elbows slightly in front of the bar, and core braced
- Slightly bend your knees and quickly extend your hips and knees to drive the bar overhead
- Fully extend your arms overhead, then slowly lower the bar back to shoulder level
- Maintain a neutral spine and avoid excessive leaning
