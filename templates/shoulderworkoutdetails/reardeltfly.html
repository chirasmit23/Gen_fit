Rear Delt Fly

Description

The Rear Delt Fly is a key isolation exercise for targeting the posterior deltoids. It helps in improving shoulder balance and posture, especially useful for those who spend time doing chest-dominant workouts or desk work.

Posture Guide

- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart or sit on a bench
- Hold dumbbells with a neutral grip, arms extended downward
- Hinge forward at the hips, keeping your back flat
- Raise your arms to the sides in a wide arc, squeezing shoulder blades
- Pause at the top, then lower the dumbbells with control
- Avoid using momentum or swinging the arms
