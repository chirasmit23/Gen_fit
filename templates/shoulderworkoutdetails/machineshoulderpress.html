Machine Shoulder Press

Description

The Machine Shoulder Press is a guided resistance exercise designed to strengthen the deltoid muscles, particularly the anterior and medial heads. Using a machine provides added stability, making it ideal for beginners or for focusing on shoulder isolation with minimal lower body involvement.

Posture Guide

- Adjust the seat so that the handles are level with your shoulders
- Sit with your back flat against the pad and feet planted firmly
- Grip the handles with palms facing forward
- Press the handles upward until your arms are almost fully extended
- Slowly return to the starting position, maintaining control throughout
