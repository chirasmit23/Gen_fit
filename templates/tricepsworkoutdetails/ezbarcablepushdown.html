EZ Bar Cable Pushdown

Description

The EZ Bar Cable Pushdown is a popular triceps exercise performed at a cable station. Using the EZ bar attachment helps reduce wrist strain while maintaining a strong contraction in the triceps during each rep.

Posture Guide

- Stand facing the cable machine with feet shoulder-width apart
- Grasp the EZ bar with an overhand grip and elbows close to your torso
- Push the bar down until your arms are fully extended
- Slowly return to starting position, keeping tension on the triceps
