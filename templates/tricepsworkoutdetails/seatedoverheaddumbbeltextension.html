Seated Overhead Dumbbell Extension

Description

This exercise targets the long head of the triceps and is performed while seated. It offers a strong stretch and contraction through the full range of motion using a single dumbbell or two.

Posture Guide

- Sit upright on a bench with back support
- Hold a dumbbell overhead with both hands under the top plate
- Keep your elbows pointing forward and close to your head
- Lower the dumbbell behind your head in a controlled motion
- Extend arms back to the top without locking elbows
