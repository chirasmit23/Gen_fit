Rope Pushdown

Description

The Rope Pushdown targets the triceps using a rope attachment on a cable machine. It allows for a natural wrist rotation and a deeper contraction at the bottom of the movement.

Posture Guide

- Stand upright facing the cable machine
- Grab the rope with a neutral grip (palms facing each other)
- Keep elbows tight to your body and extend the arms down
- At the bottom, spread the rope ends to fully contract the triceps
- Return slowly to the starting position
