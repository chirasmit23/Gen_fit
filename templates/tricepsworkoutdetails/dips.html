Dips

Description

Dips are a compound upper body exercise that primarily target the triceps, chest, and shoulders. Performed using parallel bars or a dip station, they are effective for building pressing strength.

Posture Guide

- Grip the dip bars and lift your body up
- Lean slightly forward, keeping elbows close to your body
- Lower your body by bending your elbows until your upper arms are parallel to the floor
- Push through your palms to return to the top
